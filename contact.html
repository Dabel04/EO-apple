Quick Answers

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

schedule

How long does a repair take?

Most screen repairs and battery replacements are completed within 1-2 hours. More complex repairs may take 24-48 hours. We offer same-day service for many common issues.

verified_user

Do you use genuine parts?

Yes, we use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts for all repairs. Our screens, batteries, and components meet Apple's specifications and come with our 90-day warranty.

local_shipping

Do you offer shipping for repairs?

Yes! We offer free inbound shipping for repairs. Simply request a prepaid label, ship your device to us, and we'll repair and return it within 3-5 business days.

swap_horiz

What's your trade-in process?

Get an instant online quote, mail us your device with free shipping, and receive payment within 24 hours of inspection. We accept iPhones, iPads, MacBooks, and Apple Watches.
